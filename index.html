...Where it's good to be home

Building a custom home is a creative experience and an artistic expression, where ideas mixed with emotions spring to life. This is especially true for Scharf Custom Homes' president, John M. Scharf, whose artistic background includes training at the Washington University School of Fine Arts and the University of Kansas. His artist's eyes, coupled with a passionate pursuit of excellence, have made Scharf Custom Homes a respected member of St. Louis' custom home industry.

Beautiful examples of the company's creations are on display here and we invite you to take a few minutes to look them over. Behind each finished product, however, is the vital planning, preparation and careful attention to detail that make a Scharf home not only beautiful, but an enduring, highly livable expression of the owner's needs and desires. This is a primary key to the company's success-being able to translate the client's ideas into magnificent reality.

If you are seeking a custom home builder who is dedicated, experienced, and looks at custom building through an artist's eyes, contact John Scharf at Scharf Custom Homes. John will personally meet with you to discuss your ideas and requirements, and explain how the Scharf process can create the home you have always wanted.
