The Scharfs

John Scharf, President of Scharf Custom Homes, is a seasoned veteran of the homebuilding and real estate industry. He has been designing and building homes since 1983, not only in the Greater St. Louis area, but also in Florida and North Carolina.

Customer satisfaction is the primary goal for the company, and constant communication is the key. "We try to take the mystery out of custom home building by carefully exploring each step in the design and building process with our clients," John Scharf explains. "As a team, our goal is to create a home that is everything the client wants and more...where it's good to be home."

The company builds strong relationships with its clients through continual customer relations, careful planning, and scheduling, and, as letters from our clients indicate, the process becomes an enjoyable, collaborative experience. For Scharf Custom Homes, that's the only way it should be.

The Team

It takes a talented group of professionals to create a new home, each doing their assigned task in a coordinated, well orchestrated process. At Scharf Custom Homes, our team includes people who are leaders in their specialties with years of solid experience, companies with well-earned reputations for exellence in their craft. We know that when these professionals have completed their portion of the complex process, their skills will help make the finished home a masterpiece.

Our Process

A custom home starts out as a collection of ideas from you, our client, gathered in your search for the perfect home. Ocassionally, a client will already have a plan drawn by an architect, a perfectly good way to begin. More often, the process starts with an exchange of information. You provide us with your needs, desires, and expectations. We listen carefully, digest this information, then give to you in return our suggestions and cost information.

Once we are in accord, the creative process is underway. The end result of that creative process is a plan for your home, feature for feature, down to the smallest detail. You will know before one shovel of earth is turned just what your home will look like, exactly where it will sit on your lot, approximately how long it will take to complete and what the cost will be stage by stage.

Together we will select colors, flooring, wall coverings, fixtures, millwork--everything that will make your home uniquely yours. There are literally hundreds of details that must be planned and executed on a timely basis before we can call a home completed. Once construction begins, you will be kept informed of the progress as the building process procedes. This communication is very important to us-and to you--and is one big reason why we have so many satisfied customers.

Renovations

These days, many people are opting to remodel their existing home rather than buy a new one. If this is the case for you, doesn't it make sense to work with a builder that has the talent and experience to completely build a custom home from scratch? Although this would be ideal, many people mistakenly believe it would cost more for that expertise. With Scharf Custom Homes, it does not cost more.

In fact, the value is tremendous. You will get the complete focus and attention of John Scharf on your remodeling project, just the same as if he was building your home from scratch.
